The Fifth Dimension: Structural Sovereignty结构性主权 —— 当个体的主权依赖他者的主权丧失

四主权归还的是个体在身体、感受、节律与感质上的完整性。但个体的完整，从来不是在真空中实现的。当一部分人通过一个封闭的财政系统、阶层壁垒或信息茧房获得虚假的主权满足时，这种满足可能正以另一部分人的主权被剥夺为前提。The four sovereignties restore individual integrity in body, feeling, rhythm, and qualia. But individual integrity is never realized in a vacuum. When some obtain a false sense of sovereignty through a closed fiscal system, class barriers, or information cocoons, that satisfaction may come at the cost of others' sovereignty being stripped away.

结构性主权的三原则 · Three Principles of Structural Sovereignty
一、主权不可寄生（寄生不分大小） · 二、感受的真实性需要认知开放度 · 三、爱自己不应建立在系统性削弱他者主权的基础上
I. Sovereignty must not be parasitic — parasitism knows no scale. II. The authenticity of feeling requires cognitive openness. III. Self-love must not be built upon the systemic erosion of others' sovereignty.
P1
主权不可寄生
Non-Parasitic Sovereignty
一个人的身体、感受、节律与感质主权的实现，不能以制度性剥夺他人的主权为基础。如果一个系统的运作需要大量人群失去身体自主、感受被漠视、节律被压榨、感质被贬低，那么这个系统内部个体的主权是虚假的、寄生的。财政供养困局即为一例：少数人的安逸建立在多数人的耗尽之上。 寄生不分大小：一次性的撒谎构陷，与系统性的阶层壁垒，共享同一个伦理结构 —— 我的得利建立在他者被剥夺之上。One person's sovereignty cannot rest upon the institutional deprivation of another's. If a system requires massive loss of bodily autonomy, feeling, rhythm, or qualia, the sovereignty of those inside it is illusory and parasitic. The fiscal dependency trap is one example: the comfort of a few rests on the exhaustion of many.
P2
认知开放度作为主权判准
Cognitive Openness as Sovereignty Criterion
感受的真实性，不等于感受所依赖的信息基础的真实性。当感受建立在结构性无知之上（如只对标圈内人、看不到其他阶层的生存状况），感受主权被信息茧房掏空。因此主权容量的评估必须纳入认知开放度：个体是否有能力、有意愿接触并理解不同阶层、不同处境之人的真实生活？The authenticity of feeling is not the authenticity of the information it rests on. When feeling is built on structural ignorance, sovereignty is hollowed by the information cocoon. Sovereignty capacity must be assessed with cognitive openness: can the person access and understand the real lives of those in other classes and situations?
P3
爱自己的结构伦理边界
The Structural-Ethical Boundary of Self-Love
在个体层面，做自己并捍卫四主权是爱自己的必要前提（V1.1 世界观）。但当大规模个体理性选择（如「让女儿考公务员并找公务员结婚」）汇聚成集体行动时，它可能加速系统的不可持续性，最终摧毁所有人的主权。因此爱自己必须在结构伦理上保持自反：我的爱自己，是不是在系统性地制造他者的主权废墟？At the individual level, being oneself and defending the four sovereignties is necessary for self-love (V1.1). But when individual rational choices aggregate into collective action, they may accelerate systemic unsustainability and eventually destroy everyone's sovereignty. Self-love must keep structural-ethical reflexivity: is my self-love systematically producing sovereignty ruins for others?
Scope Guard · 个体层不被推翻
结构性主权不取消前四主权，也不收回 H1 护栏。一个被剥削者的感受、身体、节律、感质，其主权与真实性始终成立，绝不因结构而被贬低 —— 这正是结构批判的依据，而非例外。结构性主权只增加一条对处于结构受益位者的自反要求：不要把寄生于他人主权废墟之上的安逸，误读为自己的主权完整。受害者无需为身处不义结构而背负伦理债务。
Structural sovereignty does not override the first four, nor retract the H1 firewall. The feeling, body, rhythm, and qualia of the exploited remain sovereign and real — never diminished by structure; that is the ground of structural critique, not its exception. It adds only one reflexive demand on those in beneficiary positions: do not misread comfort parasitic on others' sovereignty-ruins as one's own sovereign integrity. The harmed carry no ethical debt for being inside an unjust structure.
Structural Sovereignty Lock · 结构性主权锁定
主权不是私人财产，而是公共善。一个人的真正主权，不可能在一个系统性地制造主权废墟的结构中独存。因此，自我学的终点不是个体主权的孤岛，而是共同生成一个主权的生态系统。
Sovereignty is not private property but a public good. Genuine sovereignty cannot survive alone in a structure that systematically creates sovereignty ruins for others. Therefore the endpoint of Self Studies is not an island of individual sovereignty, but the co-generation of a sovereignty ecosystem.
✦ · V1.3 NEW主权冲突仲裁 · 当两个主体的主权声称发生碰撞
Sovereignty Conflict Arbitration主权冲突仲裁原则 —— 当两个主体的主权声称发生直接冲突时

四主权框架赋予每个主体平等的权利。但在现实互动中，不同个体的主权声称可能直接冲突：一方声称感受被伤害，另一方也声称感受被伤害；一方用主权语言捍卫自己，另一方用主权语言攻击对方。自我学必须面对一个艰难问题：当两个主权声称不可兼得时，如何仲裁？The four-sovereignty framework grants equal rights to every subject. But in real interactions, sovereignty claims can collide directly. When two claims cannot both stand, how do we arbitrate?

仲裁原则 · The Arbitration Principle
当两个主体的主权声称发生直接冲突时，
优先保护有独立可核验事实基础且处于结构中更弱势一方的主权。
When two subjects' sovereignty claims collide directly, prioritize the claim that has an independently verifiable factual basis and belongs to the structurally more vulnerable party.
Priority Lock · SS-ARB-A1 > SS-ARB-A2 · 防滥用排序句
LOCKED：事实基础判准 A1 优先于结构位置判准 A2。A1 决定「是否发生 / 是否成立」；A2 只在 A1 未被推翻或事实仍待查时，决定「如何保护、如何恢复、如何降低二次伤害」。A2 不得被单独引用为「弱势方永远对」。弱势位置不是事实证明；强势位置也不是有罪证明。结构位置只调整仲裁敏感度，不替代事实判断。
LOCKED: Factual Basis Criterion A1 takes priority over Structural Position Criterion A2. A1 determines whether something happened or is established; A2 only determines how to protect, restore, and reduce secondary harm when A1 is not overturned or facts remain under investigation. A2 must never be cited alone as "the vulnerable party is always right." Structural vulnerability is not proof of fact; structural power is not proof of guilt. Structural position adjusts arbitral sensitivity; it does not replace factual judgment.
A1
事实基础判准
Factual Basis Criterion
主权声称是否有独立于声称者的、可被第三方核验的证据支持？通话记录、监控录像、平台数据、多方证词 —— 这些构成主权声称的「硬地面」。没有事实基础的主权声称，不因其自称「感受真实」而自动获得优先保护。Does the claim have evidence independent of the claimant, verifiable by a third party? Records, footage, platform data, multiple witnesses form the "hard ground." A claim without factual basis does not gain priority merely because it is asserted as "truly felt."
A2
结构位置判准
Structural Position Criterion
在事实基础相当或难以判定时，优先保护在结构中处于更弱势的一方。权力、信息、资源、社会资本的不对等，会影响一个人捍卫自身主权的能力。仲裁必须对这种不对等进行补偿性校正。When factual bases are comparable or undeterminable, prioritize the structurally more vulnerable party. Asymmetries of power, information, resources affect one's capacity to defend sovereignty. Arbitration must apply compensatory correction.
应用示例：外卖骑手与女白领的主权冲突。骑手声称：我被恶意构陷，扣罚、信用降分。女白领声称：我被上门骚扰，感到不安全。仲裁 ——（1）事实基础判准：骑手有通话记录、监控、平台配送数据、警方通报；女生声称最终被证实为虚假。（2）结构位置判准：骑手在平台机制中更弱势（被扣罚无申诉权、时间被算法控制、收入不稳定）。结果：骑手的主权得到优先保护与恢复。
Example: delivery rider vs. office worker. Rider claims malicious false accusation causing penalty; worker claims harassment. Arbitration: (1) Factual basis — rider has records, footage, platform data, police report; worker's claim proven false. (2) Structural position — rider more vulnerable in the platform mechanism. Result: rider's sovereignty receives priority protection and restoration.
V1.3 仲裁机制审计
主权冲突仲裁原则不是取消任何一方的主权，而是在冲突不可调和时提供裁决机制。理想状态下两个主体的主权可以共同恢复（如平台纠错、对方道歉赔偿）。但在理想不可达时，仲裁确保不出现「撒谎者利用主权语言压制受害者」的局面。仲裁的目标始终是恢复被侵犯的主权，而不是惩罚。
The arbitration principle cancels no one's sovereignty — it provides a decision mechanism when conflict is irreconcilable. Ideally both parties' sovereignty can be restored together. When the ideal is unreachable, it ensures that "liars using sovereignty language to suppress victims" cannot stand. The goal is always restoration, not punishment.
